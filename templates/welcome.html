Welcome to Study AutoMate - Created with the OpenAI API

Study AutoMate is a web app that allows you to generate flashcards from your notes.

Created by Tom Overall (2023).

It's as simple as:

  • Step 1: Select a page of notes you would like to study (PDF).
  • Step 2: Press 'Generate Flashcards'.
  • Step 3: Upload the CSV file to a flashcard app of your choice to start studying.
